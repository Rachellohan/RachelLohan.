How To Start Weightlifting Return

One of the most important yet constantly overlooked part of fitness is consistency. The goal is to make fitness a lifestyle . YOU need to talor your goals to meet the lifestyle and activites you follow. A good example is the rise of "functional training" amoung parents who want to be active with their kids. I created this logo "WHOOSIE" because lifting weights is fun and should not be intimidating. And you should be able to look at a "whoosie whatsit" looking machine and not feel out of place. Personally, I got into weights after fracturing my right hip at 16 years old. I could only train upper body and so for six months that's what I did. I fell in love with the process and the milestones. I want to share my love of weightlifting. Here are some of the plans that I have created for myself. Also, don't forget to take a rest day:)

*I am not a personal trainer/Perform at your own risk.
